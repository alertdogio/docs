<Note>
  Notify to Group can receive up to 20 messages per minute. For higher limits, please choose [Notify to Telegram Custom Bot](/en/notification-channels/custom-telegram-bot)
</Note>


## Setup Steps

<Steps>

<Step title="Create Group and Get Chat ID">
  Open Telegram, create a new group (or Channel)

  <Frame caption="Create a Telegram Group">
  <img
    src="/images/notification-channels/telegram/telegram-create-group.png"
    alt="Create Group"
  />
</Frame>

Search for MCT_CLUB_BOT and add it to the group

  <Frame caption="Search MCT_CLUB_BOT">
  <img
    src="/images/notification-channels/telegram/telegram-search-mct-bot.png"
    alt="Telegram search MCT_CLUB_BOT"
  />
</Frame>

 <Frame caption="Add MCT_CLUB_BOT to Group">
  <img
    src="/images/notification-channels/telegram/telegram-add-mct-bot.png"
    alt="Add MCT Bot to Telegram group"
  />
</Frame>
After successfully added, MCT Bot will automatically send the Chat ID in the group, copy the Chat ID for later use

  <Frame caption="Telegram Group Chat ID">
  <img
    src="/images/notification-channels/telegram/telegram-copy-chat-id.png"
    alt="Telegram group Chat ID"
  />
</Frame>

</Step>

<Step title="Create Notification Channel">
  Open AlertDog console, select Telegram, enter the ID and click Connect
  Open AlertDog **Add New Channel** -> **Telegram** -> **Notify to Group** -> **Enter Chat ID from previous step** -> **Connect** to confirm
   <Frame caption="Create Notification Channel">
  <img
    src="/images/notification-channels/telegram/telegram-group-notification.png"
    alt="Create notification channel"
  />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Telegram Group Channel Created">
After successful connection, the channel is added successfully, the notification channel list will display this notification channel
<Frame caption="Notification Channel Created">
  <img
    src="/images/notification-channels/telegram/telegram-group-created.png"
    alt="Telegram group channel created"
  />
</Frame>
<Check>You have successfully completed Telegram notify to group setup</Check>
</Step>

</Steps>



## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="How to unbind Telegram?">
  Find the Telegram item in the notification channel list, click "Delete" to unbind.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I invite the Telegram bot to multiple groups for binding?">
 Yes.
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
