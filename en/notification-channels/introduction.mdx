## Overview

AlertDog supports multiple notification channels, allowing you to receive monitoring alert messages in the most convenient way.

## Supported Notification Channels

AlertDog supports 9 notification channels to meet different scenarios and user needs.

### Instant Messaging Platforms

<CardGroup cols={2}>

  <Card title="Discord Channel" icon="discord" href="/en/notification-channels/discord">

## How to Add Notification Channels

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Console">
    After logging into AlertDog, click "Console" in the top left corner to enter the management interface
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Notification Channels">
    Find the "Notification Channels" option in the left menu
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Channel">
    Click "Add Notification Channel" and select the notification method you need
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Configuration">
    Complete the corresponding configuration or binding operation as prompted
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can add multiple notification channels simultaneously and select which channels to use for notifications when creating monitoring. Different monitoring tasks can be configured with different notification channel combinations.
</Tip>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Can I use multiple notification channels simultaneously?">
  Yes. You can configure multiple notification channels for the same monitoring task. When monitoring triggers, notifications will be sent to all configured channels simultaneously.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Which notification channel is the fastest?">
  Telegram and Webhook have the fastest push speeds, usually at the millisecond level. If you need the fastest notification response, it's recommended to use Telegram personal notification or Webhook.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Which notification channel should enterprise users choose?">
  Enterprise users can choose based on team usage habits:
  - FeiShu: Suitable for enterprises using ByteDance office suites
  - DingTalk: Suitable for enterprise users in the Alibaba ecosystem
  - WeChat Work: Suitable for enterprise users in the Tencent ecosystem
  - Discord Channel: Suitable for overseas teams or technical communities
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What are the message limits for notification channels?">
  Different channels have different limits:
  - Telegram Personal: Up to 30 messages per second
  - Telegram Group: Up to 20 messages per minute
  - DingTalk: Up to 20 messages per minute
  - Webhook: No limit (depends on your server)
  
  For higher limits, upgrade to VIP and use custom Telegram Bot.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
