<Warning>
  Telegram notify to personal uses a shared bot, messages may be delayed during peak on-chain activity. If your business is critical, we recommend using [Notify to Telegram Custom Bot](/en/notification-channels/custom-telegram-bot)
</Warning>

## Setup Steps

<Steps>
<Step title="Select Telegram as Notification Channel">
  Open AlertDog **Add New Channel** -> 
  Select **Telegram** as platform -> Notification name can keep default (or set to an identifiable name like "Jay's Telegram")

<Frame caption="Select Telegram">
  <img
    src="/images/notification-channels/telegram/telegram-select-platform.png"
    alt="Select Telegram"
  />
</Frame>

</Step>

<Step title="Connect and Bind">
  Click the **Click Connect** button under **Notify to Personal**, Telegram dialog will automatically pop up

  <Frame caption="Connect to Your Telegram">
  <img
    src="/images/notification-channels/telegram/telegram-click-connect.png"
    alt="Connect to your Telegram screenshot"
  />
</Frame>
Pop-up window
<Frame caption="Click Open Telegram Button">
  <img
    src="/images/notification-channels/telegram/telegram-open-app.png"
    alt="Telegram pop-up window screenshot"
  />
</Frame>

After opening Telegram, MCT Bot will automatically send you a message confirming channel creation

<Frame caption="Channel Created Success Message Screenshot">
  <img
    src="/images/notification-channels/telegram/telegram-bot-message.png"
    alt="You will receive a message confirming notification channel creation"
  />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Complete Binding">
  Return to AlertDog, the notification channel has been successfully added, the notification channel list will display your Telegram account.

<Frame caption="Screenshot of List Page After Monitor Channel Creation">
  <img
    src="/images/notification-channels/telegram/telegram-channel-created.png"
    alt="Screenshot after monitor channel creation"
  />
</Frame>
<Check>Congratulations! You have successfully bound Telegram</Check>
</Step>
</Steps>



## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="How to unbind Telegram?">
  Find the corresponding Telegram item in the notification channel list, click the three-dot icon and select "**Delete**" to unbind.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can one AlertDog account bind multiple Telegrams?">
  Yes. But the same Telegram account can only bind one AlertDog account
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
