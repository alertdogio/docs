<Note>
  Group notifications can receive up to 20 messages per minute. For higher limits, upgrade to VIP and set up a custom Bot
</Note>



## Operation Steps

<Steps>

<Step title="Get Chat ID">
  Open Telegram, create a group and invite MCT Bot

  <Frame caption="Create Group">
  <img
    src="/images/notification-channels/telegram/Group 1948.png"
    alt="Create group"
  />
</Frame>

Search for MCT_CLUB_BOT and add it to the group


  <Frame >
  <img
    src="/images/notification-channels/telegram/Group 1949.png"
    alt="Telegram search for MCT_CLUB_BOT"
  />
</Frame>

 <Frame >
  <img
    src="/images/notification-channels/telegram/Group 1950.png"
    alt="Telegram add MCT Bot to group"
  />
</Frame>


</Step>

<Step title="Copy Chat ID">
After successful addition, MCT Bot will automatically send the Chat ID in the group, copy the Chat ID

  <Frame caption="Copy Chat ID">
  <img
    src="/images/notification-channels/telegram/Group 1951.png"
    alt="Copy Chat ID"
  />
</Frame>

</Step>

<Step title="Create Notification Channel">
  Open AlertDog console, select Telegram, enter the ID and click connect
   <Frame caption="Create Notification Channel">
  <img
    src="/images/notification-channels/telegram/telegram-group-notification.png"
    alt="Create notification channel"
  />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Creation Complete">
After successful connection, the notification channel has been successfully added and will appear in the notification channel list
<Frame caption="Creation Complete">
  <img
    src="/images/notification-channels/telegram/telegram-group-created.png"
    alt="Creation complete"
  />
</Frame>

</Step>

</Steps>



## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="How to unbind Telegram?">
  Find the Telegram item in the notification channel list and click "Delete" to unbind.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I invite the Telegram bot to multiple groups for binding?">
 Yes.
</Accordion>
