## Manage Twitter Monitoring

You can manage added Twitter monitoring through the following methods:

- **Filter Group Management**: After selecting a specific group, you can modify the group name or delete the wallet group
- **Batch Operations**: Click batch operations to perform batch move to group, batch edit, batch modify notification methods, batch delete on selected Twitter accounts
- **Export**: Twitter list data supports export to Excel format
- **Monitoring History**: View received monitoring notification history on the unified monitoring history page
- **Search**: Search for specific Twitter accounts
- **Enable/Disable, Edit, Delete**: Perform enable/disable, edit, delete operations on Twitter accounts
