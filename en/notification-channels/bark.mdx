Introduction: Bark is a push tool designed specifically for iOS users, allowing AlertDog monitoring messages to be pushed to your iPhone or iPad, supporting offline notifications and custom ringtones.


<Note>
  Before using Bark notifications, you need to install the Bark App on your iOS device
</Note>

# Operation Steps

<Steps>

<Step title="Creation Complete">
After successful connection, the notification channel has been successfully added and will appear in the notification channel list


</Step>
</Steps>

## Get Bark Push Key

1. Open the Bark App on your iOS device
2. Copy the displayed push Key
3. Enter the Key into AlertDog configuration

Usage Instructions: After configuration, monitoring trigger messages will be pushed to your iOS device via APNs, receiving notifications even in screen-off or offline states.

# FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Does Bark support Android devices?">
  No. Bark is a push tool designed specifically for iOS. Android users can choose other notification methods.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I customize the push ringtone?">
  Yes. You can set custom ringtones and notification styles in the Bark App.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Does using Bark require payment?">
  The Bark App is free to use, but if you use a self-hosted server, you need to deploy it yourself.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
