<Note>
**Wallet unable to confirm authorization?**

1. Try refreshing the page, restarting the wallet extension, or restarting the browser.

2. Having multiple wallet extensions enabled simultaneously may cause authorization conflicts. It's recommended to temporarily disable other wallet extensions and keep only one wallet for sign in.

</Note>
