<Note>

</Note>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="How to unbind Telegram?">
  Find the Telegram item in the notification channel list and click "Delete" to unbind.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I bind multiple Telegram accounts?">
  Yes. However, the same Telegram account can only be bound to one AlertDog account
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
